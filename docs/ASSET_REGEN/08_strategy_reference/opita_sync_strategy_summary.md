# Opita Sync strategy summary

## Current strategic position

Opita Sync already has enough technical and product depth.

The next step is **not** to open more breadth by default.

The next step is to convert that depth into clearer product value and controlled adoption.

## Strategic focus now

1. keep the two current domains strong and legible
2. avoid opening a third vertical prematurely
3. use the product as a governed control plane, not as a generic AI engine story
4. reinforce adoption through few, repeatable, valuable use cases

## What the product stands on today

- governed corridor
- tenant activation
- two real domains
- approvals, evidence, inspection and recovery
- AI-first experience over a governed kernel

## Strategic recommendation

The product should move through **controlled adoption**, using the two existing domains as the basis for:

- product demonstration
- operational feedback
- commercial learning

## Short executive line

> Opita Sync should grow by deepening the value of its current domains, not by opening more surface area too early.
