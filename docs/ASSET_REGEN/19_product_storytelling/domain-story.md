# Domain story — regenerated narrative

## Purpose

Explain the two real domains of the product as one coherent system.

## Domain 1

- tenant configuration governance
- governs what exists and is enabled for a tenant

## Domain 2

- tenant access and delegation governance
- governs who can use, approve or delegate what exists

## Bridge statement

Domain 1 governs **what** is available.

Domain 2 governs **who** can act on it.

## Avoid

- showing them as unrelated product lines
- making domain 2 look like an optional add-on
