# Evidence and recovery story — regenerated narrative

## Purpose

Explain that Opita Sync is valuable not only because it acts, but because it remains explainable and recoverable.

## Sequence

1. change is proposed
2. preview exposes risk and approvals
3. execution stays correlated
4. evidence is stored canonically
5. inspection reconstructs the case
6. recovery remains explicit and governed

## Mandatory terms

- evidence trail
- canonical records
- inspection view
- explicit recovery
- no direct mutation outside governed paths
