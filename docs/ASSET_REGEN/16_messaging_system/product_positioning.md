# Product positioning

Opita Sync should be positioned as an **AI-first governed control plane** for operating business changes and workflows over real systems.

It is built on top of **Opita Sync Framework (OSF)**, which remains the governed kernel and canonical authority.

## What it is

- a governed control plane
- AI-first in experience
- grounded in approvals, evidence, inspection and recovery
- tenant-based in activation and usage

## What it is not

- not a universal engine
- not a generic enterprise AI platform
- not a chatbot product
- not the framework itself

## Positioning center

**control plane + governance + evidence + tenant operability**
