# Messaging matrix

| Pillar | What it means | Proof angle | Sample line |
|---|---|---|---|
| Governed control plane | The product governs changes instead of executing through opaque automation | approvals, preview, operator workspace, recovery | Operate with control, not with guesswork. |
| AI-first experience | AI reduces friction in the experience layer without replacing authority | intake, proposal, preview, guidance | AI guides the work; governance still decides. |
| Tenant operability | Value starts when a tenant becomes operable | bootstrap, baselines, catalog, connectors | Activate a tenant into a real operating state. |
| Evidence and recovery | Every meaningful path remains inspectable and recoverable | evidence trail, inspection, recovery | Move from action to evidence without losing the recovery path. |
| Role clarity | Admin, operator and approver each have explicit authority | surfaces, approvals, delegation | Give each role the authority it needs, not more. |
